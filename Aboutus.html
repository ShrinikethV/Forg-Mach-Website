About Us

History

We are in the forging industry from February 2017 specializing in Forging components by exploring the opportunities in the forging Industry.

Our sister concern M/s. Shanmugar Industries has been producing forging components to various Automobile, Tractor & Earth moving segments.

Now we have extended our forging facility and also commenced a machine shop by extending our operations in our own shed spanning 1.50 acres with a covered area of 22,000 sq.ft.

Thus, we are suitable for entire forging and machining operations in our current state.

Capabilities

We produce forged components and machined components required for Automobile, Agricultural, Power, Earthmoving Equipment, Power & Oil and other Engineering Sectors.

All the forged components would be machined at our factory itself.

We have a dedicated team of Forging and machining staff and we are proud to say that we can cater to any requirements of the industry.

Product Range

We manufacture steel forging and machining parts required for Automobile industry, Power Plants, General Engineering, Earth moving Equipment manufacturers, etc.

Components include Brake Hand Lever, Beams, Pull rods, Spacer Nut, Adaptors, Gear forgings, Rocker levers, Flanges, Jaw end, Eye end, Brake pedal pads, Cam bushes, small crank shafts, connecting rods, Levers of various descriptions, Sleeves etc.

We also customize it as per the requirements and specifications of our valuable customers.

Certification

Our unit is a ISO 9001:2015 certified company and IATF certified unit.
